BIOGRAPHY

Anne Frank: The Biography

Completely revised new edition of Anne Frank's definitive biography, expanded to include unknown material. The book offers a thorough investigation of her life, including previously secret diary entries, letters, and notes from contemporary witnesses.

The Emperor of All Maladies: A Biography of Cancer

Riveting biography of cancer by Siddhartha Mukherjee, offering an exploration of the disease's history, from its first appearances to modern-day treatments, and presenting it as a major player in human health.

E=mc²: A Biography of the World's Most Famous Equation

This biography by David Bodanis explores Einstein's groundbreaking equation, explaining the relationship between mass and energy, and its revolutionary impact on modern physics and cosmology.

Albert Einstein: A Biography

Albrecht Folsing's biography sheds light on Einstein's extraordinary life, exploring his contributions to science and his influence on cultural and political issues of the 20th century.

Steve Jobs: The Man Who Thought Different

Riveting biography of Steve Jobs, the visionary co-founder of Apple, exploring his innovative contributions to computing, technology, and design.

Madame Curie: A Biography

Written by Ève Curie, this biography celebrates Marie Curie's groundbreaking work in radioactivity and her two Nobel Prizes, providing insight into her life and the challenges she faced.
