B T S

Icon Of K-pop

--by Adrian Beslby

"BTS: Icon of K-Pop" is a comprehensive and engaging biography that explores the rise of BTS, one of the most influential and successful K-pop groups in the world. Adrian Besley's writing is informative and accessible, making the book an enjoyable read for both fans and non-fans of BTS.
The book provides a detailed account of BTS's history, from their early days as trainees to their current status as global superstars. Besley's research is thorough, and he draws on a wide range of sources, including interviews with the group members and their team, as well as media reports and academic studies.
One of the standout features of this book is its ability to balance fan-friendly content with more in-depth analysis and critique. Besley is clearly a fan of BTS, but he also approaches the group's music and message with a critical eye, exploring the ways in which they reflect and challenge societal norms and expectations.
The book also provides a valuable insight into the K-pop industry and its global impact. Besley explores the history and evolution of K-pop, as well as its cultural and economic significance in Korea and beyond.
The writing style is engaging and easy to follow, making the book an enjoyable read for fans of all ages. The book also includes a range of photographs and illustrations, which add to the overall visual appeal of the book.
Overall, "BTS: Icon of K-Pop" is a must-read for anyone interested in BTS, K-pop, or popular culture more broadly. With its comprehensive research, engaging writing style, and fan-friendly content, it's a book that will appeal to a wide range of readers.
Rating: 5/5 stars
Recommendation: This book is perfect for fans of BTS and K-pop, as well as anyone interested in popular culture and music. It's also a great choice for readers who are looking for a comprehensive and engaging biography of one of the most influential and successful K-pop groups in the world.
