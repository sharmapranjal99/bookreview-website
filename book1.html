A.P.J ABDUL KALAM

a life

--by Arun Tiwari

Introduction:

"APJ Abdul Kalam: A Life" is a biography written by Arun Tiwari, a close associate and co-author of Dr. Kalam's previous books. The book provides an intimate and detailed account of Dr. Kalam's life, from his childhood to his presidency.

Early Life:

The book begins with Dr. Kalam's childhood in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. Tiwari vividly describes the humble surroundings and the struggles faced by Dr. Kalam's family. The author highlights the influence of Dr. Kalam's parents, Jainulabdeen and Ashiamma, on his early life and the values they instilled in him.

Education and Career:

Tiwari chronicles Dr. Kalam's academic journey, from his school days to his graduation from Madras Institute of Technology. The author provides insights into Dr. Kalam's early interests in science and technology, and his passion for learning. The book then delves into Dr. Kalam's career as a scientist and engineer at the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) and the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO). Tiwari provides detailed accounts of Dr. Kalam's contributions to various projects, including the development of the Agni missile and the Pokhran-II nuclear test.

Presidency:

The book covers Dr. Kalam's presidency from 2002 to 2007. Tiwari provides an insider's perspective on Dr. Kalam's initiatives and policies, including his focus on education, healthcare, and rural development.

Personal Life:

Throughout the book, Tiwari also provides glimpses into Dr. Kalam's personal life, including his relationships with his family and friends. The author highlights Dr. Kalam's simplicity, humility, and kindness, which earned him the respect and admiration of people from all walks of life.

Conclusion:

"APJ Abdul Kalam: A Life" is a comprehensive and engaging biography that provides a detailed account of Dr. Kalam's life and achievements. Tiwari's writing is lucid and accessible, making the book an enjoyable read for both enthusiasts and casual readers. The book is a testament to Dr. Kalam's remarkable life and legacy, and serves as an inspiration to readers of all ages. It is a must-read for anyone interested in the life and times of one of India's most beloved leaders.
