NON-FICTION

A Slip of the Keyboard: Collected Non-Fiction

Terry Pratchett has earned a place in the hearts of readers the world over with his bestselling Discworld series. This collection brings together his finest non-fiction writing, including serious and surreal pieces on topics from mushrooms to Gandalf's love life.

Human Body Theater: A Non-Fiction Revue

Welcome to the Human Body Theater, where each biological system of the human body is explored through a theatrical revue. Maris Wicks creates a fun and informative comic format to introduce young and old readers to the wonders of human biology.

Non-Fiction

Chuck Palahniuk's essays offer a unique and unsettling glimpse into alternative cultures, fame, and personal experiences. From encounters with celebrities like Marilyn Manson to reflections on his father’s murder, these essays are daring and original.

Kapuściński non-fiction

This biography of Ryszard Kapuściński, written by Artur Domosławski, provides an extensive analysis of his life and work, exploring the socio-political events of the last half-century and the contradictions surrounding the acclaimed journalist and reporter.

The View from the Cheap Seats: Selected Non-fiction

Neil Gaiman's collection of speeches, essays, and introductions provides insight into his views on literature, art, and culture. It includes viral speeches like "Make Good Art" and reflections on artists such as Terry Pratchett, Lou Reed, and Ray Bradbury.

Never Eat Alone: And Other Secrets to Success, One Relationship at a Time

Tahl Raz's guide explores the power of networking and building relationships. Offering practical advice, this book helps readers understand the importance of connections and how they contribute to personal and professional success.

The Schoolgirl, Her Teacher and his Wife

This investigative book by Rebecca Hazel uncovers the dark story of Chris Dawson, a teacher who became involved in the disappearance of his wife, Lynette, and the efforts to bring him to justice years later through investigative reporting.

Unexplained Mysteries Of The World: A Non-Fiction Collection About True Hauntings, Lost Civilizations, Alien Contact & Other Paranormal Enigmas

This collection explores some of the most captivating unexplained mysteries, from lost civilizations to UFO sightings. It invites readers to ponder the unexplained and consider the mysteries that still puzzle humanity today.
